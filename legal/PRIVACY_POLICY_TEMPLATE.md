## What we collect

**Account information:** name, email address, and password (stored
hashed by Firebase Authentication — we never see or store your plaintext
password).

**Content you upload:** video files, titles, descriptions, and category
tags you submit.

**Usage data:** view counts, upload timestamps, and which tier your
account is on.

**Payment information:** if you subscribe to a paid tier, payment is
processed entirely by Stripe. We receive confirmation that a payment
succeeded and your subscription status — we never receive or store your
card number, CVV, or full billing details.

## How we use it

- To operate your account and display your content
- To process payments for paid tiers (via Stripe)
- To moderate content for compliance with our Terms of Service and
  Content Guidelines, including automated scanning (via Cloudinary's
  moderation services) and human review
- To respond to support requests or copyright/abuse reports

## Automated content scanning

Uploaded videos are automatically scanned by a third-party moderation
service ([WebPurify / Amazon Rekognition — name whichever you actually
enable]) before becoming publicly visible. This scan analyzes video
content for policy violations; it does not analyze content for any
purpose other than the moderation decision described here.

## Third parties we share data with

- **Firebase (Google):** authentication and database hosting
- **Cloudinary:** video file storage, delivery, and automated content
  moderation
- **Stripe:** payment processing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
